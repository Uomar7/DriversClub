About us

Drivers club is a platform for drivers themselves.
As a driver you can sign up and get to advertise yourself either
as a taxi driver,bus driver or driver for hire.
The idea came out of the problem that some people got to
driving schools but sit at home with their licenses.
At Drivers club,we give the drivers the oppurtunity to make
their profile and be able to be viewed by potential clients.
Where be a sacco or a normal client,can view drivers profile
and and select the driver of their choice then contact them.
