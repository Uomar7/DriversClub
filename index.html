About

Drivers club is a platform that serves as a link between drivers,employers in drivers saccos and clients. Employers in the sacco are allowed to be the admins and they can be the ones to monitor activities of drivers or asign them duties.
A driver who is trained is allowed to sign up to the club and wait for approval from the employees.If approved they can now be made available for hire or assigned duties by the employees.
A client is allowed to look up a driver,view their profiles and select the driver of their choice to hire.
